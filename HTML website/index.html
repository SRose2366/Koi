About Us

We are Koii Streetwear, we make videos all about fashion. Our channel gives entertainment, information, styles and more things you can name that is all about fashion. Our main goal is to share this passion to the world through this platform. To introduce everyone to the big industry that runs through eternity. We are currently exploring the wonders of YouTube after successfully making our website to spread this world.
Koii Streetwear aims to introduce people to the fashion industry using this platform. We create a creative space where people can build their passion, happiness and dream. And leave them happy and inspired. Our channel is inspired by streetwear fashion hence our brand name. Which is an ode to the trendy and more on freedom, self expression, and insignificant style.

Our Journey

Our journey on YouTube started during our first quarter in our grade 9 year. We studied about YouTube, content types, and making a website. And applied all of those on making our website and video content for YouTube. We started on choosing channel type/ channel content. We picked Fashion content for our channel because we feel that all of us in our group can relate to it more than other content like gaming or mukbang. We also determine who our target audience is and how our content affects our viewers. And after determining all of them, during quarter 2 of school we put all our research on our website. And during this quarter it also helped us improve our website making with Javascript and designing our websites.

For the third quarter of our school year, this is when we start making YT videos and we also started our journey to wander this platform. We first plan our video and we also make our story board. Once all of the content and everything else is ready. We started filming and making our video and posting it to the platform. This 4th quarter, the last quarter in our S.Y. We studied YT analytics and SEO to improve our video/channel ratings. And how we can view other channels' analytics and reflect on what we can change. After that we release our second video on our channel with the learnings we get from the past lesson. Now we are telling you our journey on YouTube. It is all about learning new things and making our knowledge and imagination into reality. This journey for us is really fun because we get to do new things and do it for the grades. Just kidding, it is a year long fun journey of wandering and learning about the online world.
